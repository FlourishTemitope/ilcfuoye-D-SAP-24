About D-SAP'24

Digital Skills Acquisition Program 2024 (D-SAP'24) Theme: Innoskill- Buidling A Future in Tech is an initiave brought about by Impact Leaders Club, FUOYE. D-SAP'24 aim to impart Fuoyeites with the digital skills to help them stay competitive in the Labour Market. The program shall run Intensively for three days in Oye Campus and 3 days in Ikole Campus

The Programs to be taught are

  • Product Design (UI/UX)
  • Web Design with CMS
  • Google Workspace (Such as Excel, Appsheet etc)

    • Who can Register D-SAP'24?

    D-SAP'24 is open to every undergradutes in Fuoye. It is not department, faculty, or campus specific. It is open and free for every student that register through the D-SAP'24 official registration link.

    Benefits of Registering

  • Intensive learning/Training
  • Get certified with a certificate of completion that carries the Fuoye DVC ACAD signature
  • Gaining access to experts in the field of training
  • Networking! Networking!! Networking!!!

    • Requirements for joining D-SAP'24

    All students are required to have the requirements below.

  • All participants must be a bonafide student of Fuoye
  • Must register through the official D-SAP'24 registration link
  • Must possess a gadget (phone, tablet, or laptop)
  • Must be willing to learn
  • Must attend the 3-Day class for certificate of completion
